Our Journey

In our final year of the BSc.CSIT program, we wanted to leverage our knowledge and skill to address a real-world problem. With a shared interest in corporate recruting and technology, we were inspired to develop a system that could help recruiter to classify and rank the resumes inorder to select the best candidate for the jobs.

Why Choose Us?

Our application, Resume Classification and Ranking, utilizes cutting-edge technologies such as KNN and cosine similarity to provide accurate and efficient resume classification and ranking services. Whether you're a job seeker or a recruiter, our platform offers unparalleled insights and assistance to streamline the hiring process.
